Email Revenue Lag Pattern
92%
Email campaigns show a consistent 60-day lag between lead capture and closed revenue. Leads from email nurture sequences close at 2.1x the rate of other channels but take longer to convert.
Cross-referenced lead-pipeline.csv timestamps with closed-deals.csv close dates. Pattern holds across all 4 quarters with r-squared of 0.89.
Event Leads: 2.3x Higher LTV
88%
Leads acquired through events have a lifetime value 2.3x higher than average. They also show 40% lower churn in the first 90 days. Conference-sourced leads convert fastest.
Matched 312 event leads against closed-deals.csv renewal data. LTV calculated from initial deal + 12-month expansion revenue.
September Peak After August Conference
91%
September consistently delivers the highest ROAS (4.6x) following August conference attendance. The halo effect lasts approximately 6 weeks before returning to baseline.

85%
