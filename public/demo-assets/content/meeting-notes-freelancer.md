**2:12pm** - lead quality discussion
Tom brought up lead quality, said about 20-25% of leads are "tyre kickers"

**2:18pm** - search term review

- told Tom I'll run my negative keyword analysis tool this week, should clean up another $300-400/month in waste

**2:28pm** - PMax vs Search
walked thru the difference for Tom again (we've had this convo before):
- Search: higher intent, people actively searching for a plumber RIGHT NOW
- PMax: more awareness/discovery, showing up on Maps, YouTube, Display etc
- PMax leads tend to be earlier in the journey, more price shoppers
- but PMax is driving 30% of total lead volume so we can't just switch it off
- Tom gets it but still grumbles about the quality
